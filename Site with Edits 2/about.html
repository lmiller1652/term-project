Welcome to a fork and a pencil

Simple cooking. Joyful eating. Made for your kitchen, not a TV show.

Our Story

Welcome to a fork and a pencil, a celebration of simple, delicious home cooking. Founded in 2018, this site grew from a personal passion for cooking and sharing recipes with friends and family.

What began as a small collection of favorite recipes has grown into a community of home cooks who believe that good food doesn't have to be complicated.

What You'll Find Here

  • ✅ Approachable – No fancy techniques or hard-to-find ingredients
  • ✅ Tested – Every recipe is tested multiple times
  • ✅ Delicious – Because life’s too short for boring food

Beyond recipes, we share cooking techniques, ingredient guides, and helpful tips to make your time in the kitchen more rewarding and fun.

Our Approach

We believe cooking is one of life’s simplest pleasures. It’s not about perfection. It’s about creativity, confidence, and joy.

So pull out a fork and a pencil — and let’s cook something wonderful together.
